Bubble Watch is the Android version of our Buffett Indicator app. The iOS version is distributed as "Buffett Indicator." Both apps provide the same market indicator content and follow the same privacy practices described here. References to "the app" below apply to both.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to let you sign in and keep your account. Specifically:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and to contact you about essential service matters.
  • Google Account basic profile — when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and (if available) your profile picture from Google. We request only the basic profile and email scopes.

We do not collect your contacts, location, photos, device sensors, browsing activity, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect only to:

  • Create and authenticate your account so you can sign in securely.
  • Provide the market indicator content that the app exists to deliver.
  • Communicate with you about essential, account-related matters (for example, responding to a support or privacy request).
  • Keep the service secure and prevent abuse of accounts.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or to train any AI or machine-learning models.

4. What we do not do

  • No advertising. The apps contain no ads and use no advertising networks or ad identifiers.
  • No analytics or crash tracking. We do not collect usage analytics or crash/diagnostic data through third-party SDKs.
  • No behavioral tracking. We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • No subscriptions or in-app purchases. The apps do not process payments and do not collect billing or payment information.
  • No sale or sharing of personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising, as those terms are defined under US state privacy laws.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal data. Our apps and your account data are hosted on Google (Firebase) in the United States, which also provides "Sign in with Google" authentication. We do not share your information with anyone else, except when required by law.

6. Data retention

  • Account data (your email and Google profile basics) — kept while your account is active, and deleted within 30 days of an account-deletion request.
  • Support correspondence — kept only as long as needed to resolve your request and meet our legal obligations.
  • Backups — encrypted backup copies are overwritten on a rolling basis, within 90 days.

7. Your rights & choices

No matter where you live, you can:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate account information.
  • Delete your account and associated data (see section 8).
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

Residents of California, the other US states with comprehensive privacy laws, the EEA, the UK, and Switzerland have additional statutory rights (such as portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority). To exercise any right, email team@deepvalues.ai. We read every request and reply as soon as possible, and you will not be treated differently for exercising a privacy right.

8. Deleting your account & data

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. Email team@deepvalues.ai from the address associated with your account, with the subject "Delete my account." We will permanently delete your email and Google profile data, and confirm once it is done. Deletion is permanent and propagates to backups within 90 days.

9. Security

  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Account sign-in is handled through Google's authentication; we never see or store your Google password.
  • Access to stored account data follows the principle of least privilege and is restricted to authorized personnel.

No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard safeguards.

10. Children

The app is rated 4+ on the App Store, meaning its content is suitable for all ages. It is an informational market-indicator tool and is not directed at children as its audience. Signing in requires a Google Account, which Google requires account holders to meet a minimum age for (generally 13, or older in some regions).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (in compliance with COPPA), or under 16 where that is the applicable age of digital consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, email team@deepvalues.ai with the subject "Child data deletion" and we will remove it promptly.

11. Changes & contact

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the apps, our practices, or the law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective" date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. Your continued use of the apps after an update means you accept the revised policy.
