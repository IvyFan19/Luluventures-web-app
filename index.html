Frequently asked questions

Common questions about DeepValues.AI — what it is, who it's for, and how the research works.

What is DeepValues.AI?

DeepValues.AI is an AI-native financial assistant for individual investors. It runs multi-analyst AI research on any US-listed stock — reading every SEC filing, earnings transcript, and 13F holding — and produces a structured briefing with intrinsic value, financial deep-dive, smart-money positioning, and recent filings. Built by LuLu Ventures LLC and aligned only with the individual investor: no advertiser kickbacks, no promoted stocks, no conflicts of interest.

Who is DeepValues.AI for?

Individual retail investors who want institutional-grade stock research without paying Bloomberg or FactSet prices. Especially useful if you follow a value-investing approach — the product is built around the methods of Buffett, Munger, Greenblatt, and other legends — but it works for anyone doing fundamental analysis on US stocks.

How is DeepValues.AI different from Yahoo Finance, Seeking Alpha, or Robinhood?

Yahoo Finance gives you raw data. Seeking Alpha gives you crowd-sourced opinions. Robinhood is a broker that profits when you trade. DeepValues.AI is none of those — it's research the user pays for (so we work for you, not for advertisers), generated by AI that reads the actual SEC filings end-to-end, with sources cited on every claim. We don't sell your data, don't promote stocks, and don't get paid when you trade.

What does a research briefing include?

A typical briefing covers: business moat analysis, intrinsic value from three independent models (DCF, Owner Earnings, Damodaran method) cross-checked against peers, full financial history sourced from SEC filings, smart-money positioning (which of 29 tracked legendary investors hold the stock and how that's changed quarter over quarter), and a How-It-Makes-Money Sankey diagram. Every claim links back to the source filing or transcript.

What is Buffett Lens?

Buffett Lens is a voice-and-text conversational AI grounded in a specific company's public disclosures. Ask it anything — "how did revenue mix change last quarter?" or "what did management say about AI capex on the last call?" — and it answers with citations to the exact filing or transcript section. Voice audio is processed in real time through Microsoft Azure Speech Services and never stored.

Is DeepValues.AI investment advice?

No. DeepValues.AI is an AI research and education product, not a registered investment adviser. AI briefings are factual research and analysis; they are not personalized investment recommendations. You are responsible for your own investment decisions. Every output cites its source so you can verify what the AI tells you.

What does it cost?

Basic is free forever (30 credits per week, 7-stock watchlist, Buffett Lens text plus 3 voice queries). Plus is $9.99/month with a 7-day free trial (15-stock watchlist, track 10 gurus, unlimited Buffett Lens text). Pro is $29.99/month (100 credits/week, unlimited watchlist, all 29 gurus with real-time alerts, 30 minutes of voice per month). API access is $99/month for developers and advisors. Credit top-up packs are available.

Is my data private?

Yes. We don't sell personal data. We don't use your inputs or chats to train any AI model (ours or our vendors'). Voice audio is never stored — it's streamed to Microsoft Azure for real-time transcription only, then discarded. You can export or delete your account in one click. Full details in the Privacy Policy.
